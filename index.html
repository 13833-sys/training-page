Monday

Monday practice will be mostly about conditioning. We will warm up, practice on moves that not everyone does well on, we then will do live wrestling, followed by a circuit that has sprints, jump rope, pull ups, sit ups, etc.

Tuesday

Tuesdays are our technique focused days, you will learn new moves and will improve the mover you are not doing so good as well as improving the moves you do best.

Wednesday

Wednesdays we will have a combination of monday and tuesday, at the beginning we will be focused on getting better technique wise, and towards the end of the practice we will live wrestle and end with some conditioning

Thursday

Thursdays we will do circuits, the circuits will have exercises like jumping the rope, pull ups, sit ups, military press, etc. We then will have a break and then lift heavy wheights.

Friday

Fridays are going to be the "easiest" day of the week, we will focus on polishing our moves, and after practice we can play some games, information about the tournament or meet will be given on fridays

Saturdays

Saturdays are the days we have tournaments or meets, give it your all and have fun! if there is no tournament or meet on a saturday you will be notified the day before either on practice or as a reminder sent to your email.
